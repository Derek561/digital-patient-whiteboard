# Digital Patient Whiteboard Outreach Refocus v0.2

## Primary Goal

The Digital Patient Whiteboard is primarily an outreach and pre-admission movement board.

The main purpose is to show who is being worked on before admission, where the lead originated, where the person is currently located, whether detox placement is needed or completed, whether the person is expected to come to the program after detox, who owns follow-up, what is blocking movement, and when the next action is due.

## Core Operating Question

Who are we working on, where are they right now, where did they come from, what detox/referral path are they in, and what has to happen to get them to us?

## What This Means

The system should prioritize outreach and pre-arrival visibility before focusing on admission-day logistics.

Admission, transition, and aftercare tracking remain part of the lifecycle, but the primary dashboard should prioritize prospective patient movement before arrival.

## Primary Board Lanes

1. New Inquiry / Lead
2. Contact Attempt
3. Screening / Qualification
4. Detox Needed
5. Referred to Detox
6. Currently in Detox
7. Expected From Detox
8. Scheduled Admission
9. Arrived / Intake
10. Admitted
11. Did Not Admit / Lost
12. Aftercare Follow-Up
13. Closed

## Key v0.2 Fields

- Lead / Patient Display Name
- Lead Source
- Referral Source Name
- Current Location / Setting
- Detox Needed
- Detox Referred To
- Current Detox
- Expected From Detox
- Expected To Admit After Detox
- Target Program
- Outreach Owner
- Next Follow-Up Due
- Conversion Status
- Blocker
- Next Action
- Operational Notes

## Boundary

This remains separate from Kipu, CRM, and Oceanside Housing census.

It is not a clinical chart, not a full CRM, and not a housing census replacement.

It is a visibility layer for outreach movement, referral pathway tracking, next-action ownership, and operational accountability.

## v0.2 Build Direction

The current v0.1 app foundation will remain in place.

The next build should adjust the board lanes, dashboard counts, and add-card form to better reflect outreach movement and detox pathway tracking.
